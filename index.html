Sun and Moon

Sun

In alchemic and Hermetic traditions, suns are used to symbolize a variety of concepts,
much like the sun in astrology. Suns can correspond to gold, citrinitas, generative masculine principles, imagery of "the king",
or Apollo, the fiery spirit or sulfur,the divine spark in man,nobility, or incorruptibility. Recurring images of specific solar motifs
can be found in the form of a "Dark" or "Black Sun", or a green lion devouring a sun
